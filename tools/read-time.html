Why Estimate Reading Time?

Whether you’re a blogger optimizing user experience, a student managing study time, or a professional skimming reports, knowing how long a piece takes to read improves planning and engagement. The Read Time Estimator uses the industry-standard 200 words-per-minute benchmark to give you an accurate duration — helping you allocate time wisely.

Our tool respects your privacy: paste any text, and the analysis happens entirely in your browser. No snippets are saved, logged, or sent to servers. This makes it safe for confidential documents, unpublished drafts, or personal notes.

Used by content creators to set reader expectations and by learners to balance workloads, this utility is 100% free, requires no sign-up, and loads instantly. It’s a small tool that delivers big value — with zero compromise on security or simplicity.
