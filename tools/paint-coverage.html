Paint Like a Pro — Without the Waste

Buying too much paint wastes money; too little means extra trips to the store. DailyUtil’s Paint Coverage Calculator uses standard coverage rates (10m² per liter) to tell you exactly how much paint you need for your project — based on wall area and number of coats.

Enter your total wall area (length × height × walls), select metric or imperial, and choose coats. Get instant results in liters or gallons. All calculations happen in your browser — no room dimensions are saved or shared, making it safe for your renovation plans.

Free and ad-light, this tool works instantly. Premium users unlock a "Paint Planner" that recommends brands based on your location and saves your color palette — because great results start with the right prep.
