Design for Everyone with WCAG Compliance

Over 2.2 billion people live with vision impairments. The Color Contrast Checker ensures your text is readable by verifying it meets Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). Whether you’re a developer, designer, or content creator, this tool helps you build inclusive digital experiences that comply with global standards.

Our calculator uses the official luminance formula to compute contrast ratios. Pick any text and background color, and instantly see if it passes AA (minimum) or AAA (enhanced) accessibility levels. All computations happen in your browser — no design files are uploaded or stored.

Free and open to all, this utility supports ethical design without compromising privacy. No sign-ups, no watermarks, no hidden costs — just a fast, reliable way to make the web more accessible.
