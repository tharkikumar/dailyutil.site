Laundry Without the Guesswork

Overloading your washing machine causes poor cleaning, excess wear, and even breakdowns. Underloading wastes water, energy, and time. DailyUtil’s Laundry Load Converter uses average clothing weights to tell you exactly how many items fit in your machine — so you get clean clothes and a longer-lasting washer.

Just select your machine capacity (e.g., 7kg) and clothing type — and get an instant count. All processing happens locally in your browser; no laundry data is saved or shared. This makes it safe for everyday use, whether you're a college student or a busy parent.

Free and private, this tool requires no sign-up. Premium users unlock a "Laundry Planner" that suggests detergent amounts and eco-friendly cycles — because doing laundry should be efficient, not frustrating.
