Beat Inflation at the Grocery Store

With rising food costs, comparing unit prices is the smartest way to stretch your budget. DailyUtil’s Grocery Price per Unit Calculator instantly shows you which item gives more value — whether you're comparing 500g vs 1kg bags of rice or 1L vs 2L bottles of oil.

Enter price and quantity for two items, select units (grams, liters, ounces, etc.), and get a clear winner. All calculations happen in your browser — no purchase data is saved or shared, so you can shop with confidence.

Free and private, this tool requires no sign-up. Premium users unlock "Smart Shopper" mode — use your phone camera to scan receipts (client-side AI) and track the best prices over time — because saving money should be effortless.
