Wake Up Refreshed with Sleep Cycle Science

Sleep occurs in 90-minute cycles. Waking during light sleep (at the end of a cycle) reduces grogginess. DailyUtil’s Sleep Alarm calculates ideal bedtimes based on your desired wake-up time — helping you rise feeling rested, not exhausted.

Your wake time is processed locally — no data is stored or shared. This makes it safe for personal use without privacy concerns. The tool suggests up to 6 bedtime options (1–6 cycles before wake time), giving you flexibility.

Free, science-backed, and ad-light, this alarm supports better sleep hygiene — with no sign-up, no tracking, and no hidden costs.
