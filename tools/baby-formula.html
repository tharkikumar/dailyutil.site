Feed with Confidence

Mixing baby formula requires precision — too much powder can cause constipation, too little can lead to malnutrition. DailyUtil’s Baby Formula Converter uses real brand scoop sizes to give you accurate milliliter or ounce measurements, so you never guess.

All calculations happen in your browser — no feeding data is saved, logged, or shared. This makes it safe for sensitive use at 3 AM, in hospitals, or while traveling.

Free and private, this tool requires no sign-up. Premium users can unlock a "Baby Care Log" to track feedings locally (encrypted) and print weekly reports for pediatricians — because your baby deserves the best, without privacy trade-offs.
