Optimize Your Writing with Detailed Text Analysis

Whether you’re a student meeting word counts, a blogger targeting readability, or a professional crafting reports, the Word Counter provides instant metrics: word count, character count (excluding spaces), and a Flesch-based readability score. This helps you tailor content to your audience’s comprehension level.

Your text is analyzed locally — no snippets are saved, logged, or shared. This makes it safe for confidential drafts, unpublished work, or personal journaling. The readability score ranges from 0–100, with higher values indicating easier reading (ideal for general audiences).

Used by writers worldwide, this free utility requires no sign-up, displays minimal ads, and respects your privacy — delivering professional-grade insights in seconds.
