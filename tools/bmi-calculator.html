Understand Your BMI Category

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a quick screening tool for weight categories (underweight, normal, overweight, obese). While not a diagnostic of body fat or health, it’s widely used by healthcare providers and fitness programs. DailyUtil’s BMI Calculator gives instant, private results based on your height and weight.

All calculations happen in your browser — no measurements are saved or transmitted. This ensures your health data stays yours alone. The tool uses WHO standards for categorization, providing context alongside your numerical result.

Free, simple, and ad-light, this utility promotes health awareness without data harvesting — no accounts, no tracking, and full transparency.
