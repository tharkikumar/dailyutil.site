Why Accurate Cooking Conversions Matter

Baking and cooking rely on precise measurements — and cups are not universal. A cup of flour weighs 120g, but a cup of sugar is 200g. Using the wrong conversion leads to dry cakes, dense bread, or failed recipes. DailyUtil’s Cooking Unit Converter uses ingredient-specific densities to give you accurate grams, milliliters, or volume measures — all in your browser, with no data collection.

This tool is essential for home bakers, meal preppers, and anyone adapting recipes from American (cups) to metric (grams) systems. Whether you're making cookies for your kids or hosting a dinner party, this free utility ensures your dish turns out perfectly — every time.
