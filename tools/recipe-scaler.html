Never Ruin a Recipe Again

Scaling recipes isn't just about multiplying numbers — salt, baking powder, and spices need special handling to avoid overpowering your dish. DailyUtil’s Recipe Scaler uses culinary best practices to adjust ingredients intelligently, ensuring your scaled cake rises perfectly and your soup isn't too salty.

This tool is a lifesaver for dinner parties, holiday meals, or meal prepping for the week. Simply enter your original servings, desired servings, and list ingredients — and get an instantly scaled version. All processing happens in your browser; no recipes are saved or shared.

Free and private, this utility works instantly with no sign-up. Premium users can unlock "Smart Scale" mode (which auto-adjusts leavening agents) and export results as a printable shopping list — because feeding people should be joyful, not stressful.
