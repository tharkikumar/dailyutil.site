Take Control of Your Energy Bills

With rising electricity costs, understanding your appliance usage is key to saving money. DailyUtil’s Electricity Cost Calculator shows you exactly how much your AC, fridge, or heater costs to run — per hour, day, or month — so you can make informed decisions.

Enter your appliance’s wattage (found on the label), daily usage, and local electricity rate to get an instant breakdown. All calculations happen in your browser — no data is stored or shared, ensuring your usage habits stay private.

Free and ad-light, this tool works instantly with no sign-up. Premium users unlock "Energy Saver Mode" — compare appliance costs (e.g., "AC vs fan") and get monthly forecasts — because saving money should be simple and private.
