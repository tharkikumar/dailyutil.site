Check Text Similarity with Full Privacy

While not a full plagiarism detector (which requires internet databases), this tool compares two text blocks for word overlap — helping students self-check paraphrasing or editors verify originality. It’s a transparency-first approach: your content never leaves your browser.

Paste your draft and a source text, and get a similarity percentage. High overlap (>25%) suggests revision is needed. This is ideal for initial screening before submitting work, with zero risk of your text being stored or indexed by third parties.

Free and ethical, this utility complements academic integrity without surveillance — no sign-up, no logs, and no commercial use of your data.
