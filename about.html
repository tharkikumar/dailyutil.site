About DailyUtil

Our Mission

In a world of bloated apps and data-hungry services, DailyUtil offers a better way: simple, private, and instantly useful tools that work in your browser — no signups, no uploads, no tracking.

Why We Built This

We’re developers, students, and everyday users who got tired of:

  • Tools that require email signups for basic features
  • Sites that upload your files to unknown servers
  • Ad-cluttered interfaces that slow down simple tasks

So we built DailyUtil: a clean, fast, and ethical alternative.

Our Promise

Every tool on this site is:

  • ✅ 100% client-side — your data never leaves your browser
  • ✅ Free forever — no paywalls, no trials
  • ✅ Open and transparent — no hidden tracking

Join Us

Use our tools, share them with friends, or contact us with suggestions. Together, we can make the web more private and useful.
