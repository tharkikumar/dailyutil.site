Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2025

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

DailyUtil.site is built on a simple principle: your data belongs to you. We provide free online tools with a strict privacy-first approach — no tracking, no signups, no hidden data collection.

2. What We Don’t Collect

Our tools run entirely in your browser. We do not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit your files, passwords, or personal data
  • Use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • Log your IP address, tool usage, or inputs
  • Share any information with third parties

3. Third-Party Services

Some tools use privacy-safe APIs:

  • HaveIBeenPwned: Used for password/email breach checks via k-anonymity (only partial hash data is sent)
  • PDF.js: Open-source library for PDF text extraction (runs 100% locally)

These services do not receive your full inputs, and we do not control their privacy policies.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to keep DailyUtil free. Ad partners may use anonymous cookies for ad personalization. You can opt out by using a privacy browser (Brave, Firefox) or ad blocker.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use implies acceptance.

Questions? Reach out via our Contact page.
