Privacy Policy for DailyUtil.site

Last updated: December 12, 2025

DailyUtil.site ("we", "our") provides free online tools with a strict privacy-first approach.

1. No Data Collection

Our tools (e.g., PDF converter, password checker, water calculator) run entirely in your browser. We do not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit your files, passwords, or personal data
  • Use cookies for tracking
  • Log your IP address or tool usage

2. Third-Party Services

Some tools use privacy-safe APIs (e.g., HaveIBeenPwned for password checks) using k-anonymity — your full password is never sent.

3. Ads

We use Google AdSense to keep DailyUtil free. Ad partners may use anonymous cookies for ad personalization. You can opt out via your browser settings.

4. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use implies acceptance.
